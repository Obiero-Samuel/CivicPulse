How CivicPulse Works

A quick guide to getting results for your community.

1

Submit a Report

Describe the issue, pick a category and ward, drop a map pin. Your report gets a unique tracking number instantly.

2

Auto-Routing

CivicPulse automatically assigns your report to the correct authority — KURA for roads, Nairobi Water for pipes, KPLC for lighting, etc.

3

Track Progress

Check My Reports to see status updates. Authority officers post notes as they work on your issue.

4

Upvote & Escalate

Upvote reports that affect you too. Reports with many upvotes get escalated automatically, putting pressure on the responsible authority.

🗺 Authorities & What They Handle

🚰 Nairobi Water — Pipes, sewers, drainage
🛣 KURA — Roads, potholes, signage
💡 KPLC — Street lights, power
🗑 Public Health — Garbage, sanitation
🌳 Environment — Pollution, dumping
🏗 NCA — Illegal construction
🏛 NMS — Public facilities, parks
