Built for Accountability

Every feature is designed to close the gap between citizens and the authorities responsible for their civic infrastructure.

📍

Geo-Tagged Reports

Pin civic issues to your exact location on an interactive Leaflet map. Reports include ward, GPS coordinates, and address for precise routing.

🔄

Smart Auto-Routing

Each report is automatically assigned to the correct government authority based on its category — no manual triage needed.

⏱

7-Day Auto-Escalation

Unresolved reports are automatically escalated after 7 days. No authority can ignore a problem without it becoming public.

📊

Analytics Dashboard

Admins see real-time charts — status distribution, category breakdowns, authority performance, and weekly accountability reports.

🔔

Real-Time Updates

Socket.IO powers live notifications. When an officer updates your report, you see it instantly — no page refresh needed.

🗳

Community Upvoting

Residents upvote issues that affect them most. High-vote reports surface faster, creating democratic pressure for resolution.

How It Works

From report to resolution in four simple steps.

1

Report an Issue

Submit a report with title, description, category, and location. The system routes it to the right authority automatically.

2

Authority Responds

The assigned officer reviews the report, updates its status, and adds resolution notes visible to the reporter.

3

Track Progress

Follow your report through every status change — from open to in-progress to resolved. Full history, fully transparent.

4

Hold Accountable

If no action is taken within 7 days, the report auto-escalates. Public analytics expose authority performance.
